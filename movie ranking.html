The Best Movies According To Kelson

My Top 3 Movies of all time

John Q

An emotional, heart-wrenching story that not only takes viewers on a journey within their own souls, but also opens up their eyes to the way the health care system works.

Black Panther

Firstly, the film features a predominantly Black cast and crew, which was groundbreaking for a major superhero blockbuster. This representation was celebrated as an important step towards more diverse and inclusive storytelling in Hollywood.

The Afrofuturistic world of Wakanda, the fictional African nation that is home to the Black Panther, was also praised for its richly imagined culture, technology, and social structure. This provided a refreshing alternative to common Western depictions of Africa.

Finally, the film's stunning visual design, rousing musical score (featuring artists from the West Coast like Kendrick Lamar, Mozzy, and SOBxRBE), and well-choreographed action sequences contributed to an immersive and entertaining cinematic experience that audiences responded to positively.

Nightcrawler

Thanks to Gyllenhaal's quirky and unhinged performance, this is a volatile ride worth taking. Fresh score. A pulse-pounding thriller set on the streets of Los Angeles, Nightcrawler stars a phenomenal Jake Gyllenhaal as a man who rises to fame by recording violent events in the middle of the night.
